ITCH.IO YouTube - OST

Orbsort is an old game I made while I was in high school. I still look back on it fondly for being my first full release and still being high quality. I did all the work; gameplay, graphics, audio.

In Orbsort, your goal is to sort colored circles in groups of four. You can take as long as you like in the standard Infinite mode, but you get more points if you can make "combos" (capsules sorted in rapid succession).

It features three other modes; score attack, time attack, and local multiplayer versus. It also has several tracks of music and a few accessibility options; mainly changing the orb shape for colorblindness and increasing the combo timer.

You can play it in your browser or download it.

Nonononogram is a small nonogram (or Picross) game I made for a friend that operates along the same lines as Orbsort. It's more stripped back; where Orbsort has multiple modes, Nonononogram is simply just the Infinite mode.

You can play it in your browser or download it.

ITCH.IO

Bulletlop is a bullet hell shoot-em-up arcade game me and two friends, NathanDAWs and Robyn Starre, made for the GMTK game jam, a 4-day game development competition.

The main gimmick is that your gameplay is recorded; then, on the next loop, your gameplay is played back to you as an enemy.

We managed to get within the top 3% against 9,588 games for both categories "Enjoyment" and "Audio", though it was my friends who did the music.

I did most of the code with a lot of help from Robyn; I also did the sound design and font.

You can play it in your browser or download it.
