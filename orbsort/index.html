Info

Welcome to the Orbsort Runs List!
All of these runs are linked to a YouTube video, verified to be Of A Good Quality by me (wish volo!!)

To submit a run, use the form linked on the SUBMIT button above. To help make sure that it's really you uploading the video, upload it as unlisted and submit it as soon as you can.
Please make sure the run includes both the setup screen (or results screen of the last run) and the results screen.
You can use seed resetting as much as you want.

The leaderboard uses Combotimer × 1 and 8 Orbs.
Alternative combotimer and orb boards may be made available at popular request - please submit them so I can keep track of runs for that category regardless!

Please do not use excessive pausing in your run.
All runs that were records will have a ⬟ next to them.

Runs made by a developer of ORBSORT (as in, me, Wish Volo) will have a purple name.
All runs are dated DD.MM.YY by video date.
You can find the date or debut position of any run by hovering over one until its tooltip appears.
