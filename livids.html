002: The Tale of Frostbite

They lock the door to the room while she watches, holding her arm to her chest. Her hand was blackened. Cold, numb, frostbitten.

“What the hell is happening?” she says.

Then she starts seeing something her friend hasn’t done before.
They begin muttering something. Symbols glow on the door.

“That should hold them off,” they say once they finish.

They turn to their friend. “Alright. Mev. How’s your hand?” Then they actually look at Mev’s hand, and their red eyes widen. “Uh, that’s rhetorical.”

“Neri, tell me what the hell is going on.”

Neri purses their lips and just looks at Mev for a bit.

“I’m gonna try to help your hand, and then I’ll try to explain everything,” they say. “Alright?”

Mev stares at them. Then she takes a deep breath. “Fine.”

Neri approaches Mev slowly. “Okay.”

They start muttering something again. Her hand starts glowing.

The feeling begins to come back. She moves a finger slightly —

“Not yet,” Neri says. Mev complies.

Her hand gets warm. At first she thinks it’s the glowing.
But it keeps getting warmer. And warmer. And it burns, it burns, it burns--

“Stop, you’re burning me!” Mev shouts.
“What?” Neri says, confused, backing away. The glowing stops, but the burning persists. The hand has its feeling back, but it’s still blackened.

“It’s burning, my hand is burning!”
“Shit. The spell’s attached to you.”
“Attached?!”
“It’s created a permanent effect. Sometimes this happens. I can’t reverse it at this point.”

Mev backs away slowly.

“Mev…” Neri starts.

“What’s happening?” Mev says.

Neri lets out a breath.

“Those things are called Livids. Magical beings. Insanely dangerous. They create traps. They try to steal your soul.” A pause. “I…”

“What?”

Neri takes a breath. “Usually they stay pretty hidden, but… I don’t get why they’re so… out there. Spread out. Doing whatever the hell this is.”

Mev breathes in sharply. A siren can be heard in the distance, and both turn toward a window.

“I’m--” Neri whispers, nervous. “I’m one of them.”

They turn back to each other. Silence.

“And you’re on my side?” Mev says.

“Yes.” Neri says. “Yeah, I am.”

“Good.”

“I’m going to get you out of this. Mev, I am going to get you out of this alive. Alright?”

“Alright,” Mev whispers.
